Pital Bhakari

Pital Bhakari is a traditional Maharashtrian recipe that is made with gram flour or as we call it, Besan.t is mandatory to make in every Maharashtrian household with Bhakri, during Monsoons. Because of its simplicity and delicious taste, it has become popular not only in Maharashtra but all over India.
In Consists Of Misal :Pital,Bhakari,Onions,Lemon,Coriander.

Aamras Puri

Aamras Puri is a sweet dish featuring in the cuisine of the Indian subcontinent and made from the pulp of the mango fruit. The pulp of a ripe mango is extracted, usually by hand, and is consumed together with pooris .Sugar is also added to adjust the sweetness. It is often had at celebrations and weddings with cardamon and chopped fruits.Aamaras Puri is very testy and amaging.
In Consists Of Aamaras Puri:Puri,Aamaras,Papad
